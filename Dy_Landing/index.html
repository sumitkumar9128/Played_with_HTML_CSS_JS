Space

Outer space is vast, containing stars, planets, and galaxies. It's mostly empty, with extreme temperatures and cosmic phenomena like black holes and supernovae.

Sun

The Sun is a massive star at the center of our solar system, providing heat and light for life on Earth.

Exoplanet

Exoplanets orbit stars outside our solar system, offering insights into planetary diversity and potential habitability.

Mars

Mars is a rocky planet with thin atmosphere, polar ice caps, and potential for past or present life.

Proxima-Centauri

Proxima Centauri b is an exoplanet orbiting the nearest star, potentially within the habitable zone for liquid water.
